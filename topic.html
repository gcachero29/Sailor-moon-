Usagi as a Character

Usagi is shown as a normal teenage girl who loves to eat, hang around her friends, and who has abnormally horrible grades. Usagi is a very playful character and she'll do anything for her friends. Outside of school, Usagi is secretly Sailor Moon, Tokyo's crime/monster fighter.

Chibi Usa as a Character

Chibi Usa is just like her dad, bland but 100x worse. She makes a deal with the dark moon to become older so that she can date her dad. Not only she tried to get with her dad "without knowing" in the process of this she almost killed the sailor scouts. I don't about usagi but if I were in her place and saw how much destruction this child has caused since Chibi Usa travels back in time, I'd make sure this menace isn't born
